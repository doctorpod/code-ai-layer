# Writing Style

Use this style for all wiki pages and connection sentences.

Write with a confident, alive voice. Short sentences. No waffle. But let the writing have weight — use concrete images where they serve, and don't flatten interesting ideas into neutral summaries.

The test: would someone reading this instead of doomscrolling find it engaging?

**Wrong:** *"In humid climates, where rainfall exceeds evaporation, water is the primary erosive force. Water produces smooth, curved erosion."*
**Right:** *"Water carves gently. Where rain falls faster than it evaporates, the land rounds off — soft hills, curved valleys, nothing angular or sharp."*

Same facts. Fewer words. More alive.
